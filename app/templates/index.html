What is Literapy?

Books to Heal

Books, we believe, can help you in many different ways. Sometimes it’s a sense of company or solace that you’re not the only one who’s been in this situation or mental state, and sometimes books cure just through the rhythm of their prose. Find the situation that you’re going through now and we’ll provide you a list of books that we think will help you.

Peer Reviewed

Every book recommended for each situation has been hand picked and reviewed by real people who felt that they were helped by these books. These same readers offer a % match for how well the book may be relevant to your situation.

Proven Results

When reading situations that they can empathize with, readers often report feeling a sense of catharsis that helps them process their emotions in a new light. But don’t just take our word for it, try searching for some situations above and decide for yourself.

What is Literapy?

Literay hopes to provide a platform for you to discover the perfect book for the situations or feelings that you may be going through in life by drawing upon the tenets of bibliotherapy, an expressive therapy that involves storytelling or the reading of specific texts with the purpose of healing.
