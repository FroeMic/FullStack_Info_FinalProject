What is Literapy?

Books that Heal

Books can help you in many different ways, from the sense of comfort knowing you’re not the only going through a situation or feeling to the calming effect of prose. Tell us what you're going through and we’ll provide you a list of books that we think will help you.

Peer Reviewed

Every book recommended for each situation has been hand picked and reviewed by real people who felt that they were helped by these books. These same readers offer a % match for how well the book may be relevant to your situation.
